## Let's use the below guide to upload your files to your server!

### Step 1: Access the Control Panel
Log in to the [Control](https://control.creepercloud.io) using your credentials.

### Step 2: Navigate to the File Manager
Once logged in, navigate to the **File Manager** section from the dashboard.

### Step 3: Upload Files
1. Click on the **Upload** button.
2. Select the files you want to upload from your local machine.
3. Confirm the upload and wait for the process to complete.

### Step 4: Verify the Upload
After the upload is complete, verify that the files are correctly uploaded by checking the file list in the File Manager.
