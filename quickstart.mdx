## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Client Portal">
    Head over to our [client portal](https://control.creepercloud.com) to create your server.
    
    <Frame>
      ![Client Portal Homepage](/images/portal-homepage.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Log in to the Control Panel">
    Use the email associated with your Discord account. A password was generated when you signed up.
    
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Existing Users">
      1. Go to [Control Panel](https://control.creepercloud.io/)
      2. Enter your email address and password to log in.
      </Tab>
      
      <Tab title="Forgot Password">
        Click the **Reset Password** button on the [Password reset](https://deploy.creepercloud.io/panel) to generate a new password.
        
        <Frame>
          ![Password Reset](/images/password-reset.png)
        </Frame>
      </Tab>
      
      <Tab title="New Users">
        If you haven't registered yet:
        1. Login at [Deploy](https://deploy.creepercloud.io)
            <Frame>
               ![deploy](/images/password-reset.png)
            </Frame>
        2. Go to [Create server](https://deploy.creepercloud.io/create)
            <Frame>
               ![Create Deploy](/images/password-reset.png)
            </Frame>
        3. Then go to [Password reset](https://deploy.creepercloud.io/panel) to get your password and email
            <Frame>
               ![Password Reset](/images/password-reset.png)
            </Frame>
        4. Then go to [Control Panel](https://control.creepercloud.io/) 
            <Frame>
               ![Control Panel](/images/password-reset.png)
            </Frame>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
</Steps>
